2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit — such as your login status, preferences, and progress — making your next visit easier and the site more useful.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them
  • First-Party Cookies: Set directly by our website
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by external services we use (e.g., Stripe, YouTube)

5. Your Cookie Choices & Rights

🔧 Managing Cookies

You have the right to:

  • Accept or decline non-essential cookies via our cookie banner
  • Withdraw consent at any time through your account settings or browser
  • Delete existing cookies from your browser at any time
  • Block future cookies through your browser settings

Browser Controls

You can control cookies directly through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling certain cookies may affect:

  • Strictly Necessary: You will be unable to log in or stay signed in
  • Functional: Your learning progress and preferences will not be saved
  • Stripe: Subscription and payment pages may not function correctly
  • YouTube: Embedded videos may not play
